Linting Xue (薛林亭)

Hi, I'm a fourth year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at North Carolina State University. I am working in the Center for Educational Informatics. I work under Dr. Collin F. Lynch in the Arg lab.

My research is focused on applying deep neural network models to extract argument structures from natural language corpora. I also have conducted some research work on automatically inducing argument diagram rules from experted-graded argument diagrams and some work on social network analysis.

I did my internship at Google in Summer 2018, where I worked under Google Assistant team on the project of building deep learning models for short text/intent classification. To learn more about me, check out my experiences and some of my projects.
